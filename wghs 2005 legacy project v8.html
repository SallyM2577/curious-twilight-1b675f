▼

Funds will be managed transparently and applied directly to approved legacy projects. All income is received into a dedicated project account overseen by the MMV Year Group Treasurer.

Sponsors and partners will receive recognition and periodic project updates throughout the campaign and into the delivery phases.

The MMV Year Group remains committed to accountability, excellence, and impact — in the same spirit as the school that shaped us.
